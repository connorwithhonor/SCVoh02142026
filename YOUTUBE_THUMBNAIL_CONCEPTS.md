## Design Guidelines for All Thumbnails

### Typography
- **Main text:** Bold, sans-serif, high contrast
- **Font size:** Readable even at small sizes (mobile thumbnail preview)
- **Colors:** Blue (#1e3a8a), Amber (#f59e0b), White, Black

### Connor's Branding
- Always include Connor's face or silhouette
- Positioning: Bottom-right corner (doesn't block text)
- Style: Professional but approachable (no overly casual)

### Emotional Triggers
- **Curiosity:** "Why?" "How?" "What Changed?"
- **Urgency:** "MUST KNOW" "Alert" "Breaking"
- **Value:** "$42,000" "Save Money" "Hidden Opportunity"

### Visual Hierarchy
1. Eye-catching graphic or photo (background)
2. Main number or shocking stat (biggest element)
3. Context text (medium size)
4. Connor's branding (bottom-right)

### Thumbnail Testing
- View thumbnails at 320x180px (YouTube mobile size)
- Ensure text is readable
- Check contrast against various backgrounds

### Tools for Creation
- **Canva** - Easy templates, drag-and-drop
- **Adobe Express** - Professional designs
- **Photoshop** - Full control for custom designs
- **RemoveBG** - Remove background from Connor's photos for PNG cutouts
