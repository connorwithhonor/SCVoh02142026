#### Current Market Overview

As of today, there are **472 active residential listings** in the Santa Clarita Valley. This is a significant increase compared to the past few months when the number of listings hovered between the high 200s and low 300s. Despite this surge in inventory, buyer demand remains robust, and properties are moving quickly into escrow.

#### Market Trends and Insights

	- **Increased Inventory:** The rise to 472 active listings provides more options for buyers but has not slowed down the market. Buyer activity remains high, evidenced by the quick turnaround times for properties moving into escrow.

	- **Price Adjustments:** With 49 price changes, sellers are actively adjusting their expectations to meet market demands. This number is higher than the typical 25-30 range, indicating a dynamic pricing environment.

	- **Back on Market Properties:** 18 properties have come back on the market, often due to financing issues rather than problems with the properties themselves. This highlights the importance of buyers maintaining their financial stability throughout the purchasing process.

### Key Advice for Buyers and Sellers

	- **Selecting the Right Professionals:** Ensure that the real estate agent and lender you choose are well-equipped and experienced. Have conversations with them and research their online presence to ensure they provide valuable, educational content rather than just self-promotion.

	- **Financing Tips:** Before making any significant financial decisions, such as purchasing a new vehicle or paying off large debts, consult your lender. These actions can impact your ability to qualify for a mortgage.

	- **Upcoming Changes:** Starting in August, commission structures will shift from traditional payments to concessions. This change means commissions will be more of a contribution or offering rather than a mandatory payment, requiring mutual agreement between parties.
