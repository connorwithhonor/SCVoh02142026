## Key Metrics and Methodology

Before we examine each market area, let‚Äôs clarify the key metrics we‚Äôll be discussing:

	- **List Price:** The price at which properties are listed, representing sellers‚Äô expectations.

	- **Days on Market (DOM):** The average time a property remains on the market before a transaction is finalized.

	- **SP/LP % (Sale Price to List Price Ratio):** An indicator of how close final sale prices are to the asking prices.

	- **Bedrooms (BR) and Bathrooms (BA):** A snapshot of property sizes and functionality.

	- **Year Built:** Reflecting the age and potential renovation needs of the properties.

	- **Square Footage (Sqft):** The living area size, which influences overall value.

	- **Lot Sqft:** The size of the land associated with the property.

	- **$/SqFt:** A measure that provides a standardized value for the property‚Äôs size and quality.

	- **Carport Spaces:** Additional features that add to the property‚Äôs appeal.

This data, compiled for residential properties with statuses ranging from ‚ÄúClosed‚Äù to ‚ÄúExpired‚Äù (and other active statuses), offers an accurate view of market performance in each area. Let‚Äôs explore what these numbers reveal.
