## The Fire Law Bombshell: What AB 3074 Really Means for Your Backyard

Alright, let's cut through the bureaucratic BS. Assembly Bill 3074, signed into law faster than a Canyon Country escrow closes in a hot market, mandates an "ember-resistant zone" around every California home. We're talking a strict five-foot buffer where anything that could catch a spark has gotta go. Bark mulch? Gone. Those cute flammable shrubs hugging your foundation? Uproot 'em. Wood piles stacked like Jenga for your weekend BBQs? Relocate or regret it. And don't get me started on wooden fences ‚Äì if they're attached to the house, they might as well be kindling.
