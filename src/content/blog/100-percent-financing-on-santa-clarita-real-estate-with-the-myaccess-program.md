## Part 1: Understanding CalHFA's Core Assistance Programs

CalHFA's mission is clear: to help more Californians access affordable housing. Their DPA programs are key tools in this mission. It's vital to understand that these are loans that must be repaid, but their structure makes them incredibly advantageous. Let's break down the main players:

#### 1. CalHFA MyHome Assistance Program: The Down Payment Helper

The MyHome program provides a deferred-payment junior loan designed primarily to cover the minimum required down payment for your first mortgage.

#### 2. CalHFA MyAccess Assistance Program: Tackling Closing Costs (Often with CalPLUS)

MyAccess is specifically geared towards helping with closing costs and is most commonly paired with a CalPLUS first mortgage.

#### 3. CalPLUS First Mortgages (FHA &amp; Conventional): The Foundation

These are the primary loans covering the vast majority of the purchase price, offered with or without the MyAccess closing cost assistance.

#### 4. CalHFA ZIP (Zero Interest Program) Assistance: An Alternative for Closing Costs

Occasionally available, ZIP offers deferred closing cost assistance that accrues zero interest.

**Key Takeaway on Structure:** You typically get ONE CalHFA first mortgage (Standard or CalPLUS, FHA or Conventional) combined with ONE or sometimes TWO assistance loans (e.g., MyHome for down payment + MyAccess/ZIP for closing costs). The specific, optimal combination depends on your financial profile, the lender's offerings, and current CalHFA program availability.

#### Mortgage Credit Certificates (MCCs)

First-time homebuyers using CalHFA might *also* qualify for an MCC, issued by local agencies (not CalHFA directly, though sometimes coordinated). An MCC provides a direct federal tax *credit* (not just a deduction) for a portion of the mortgage interest paid each year, potentially saving thousands over the life of the loan. There are separate income/purchase price limits and application processes. Ask your lender early if layering an MCC with CalHFA is possible and beneficial for you.
