Canyon Country continues to be a magnet for buyers, offering a rich tapestry of housing options, from cozy townhomes to sprawling, view-oriented estates. The recent sales activity here highlights its broad appeal.

#### The Power of a View Lot

In the Robinson Ranch community, a gorgeous single-story estate on **Appaloosa Road** overlooking a golf course sold for **$1,750,000**. This custom 4-bedroom, 4-bathroom home, spanning over 3,800 square feet, features an entertainer's yard with a pool, spa, and cook station, all set against unobstructed, panoramic views. In another part of Canyon Country, a 5-bedroom home on **Crystal Heights Court** at the end of a quiet cul-de-sac sold for **$1,090,000**, over its asking price of $1,075,000. This home also boasted beautiful mountain views and owned solar, a winning combination for today's buyers.

On the more accessible end of the market, a well-maintained 3-bedroom townhouse in the Canyon Village community on **Crossglade Avenue** was listed for $499,994 and sold for **$515,000**. Similarly, a move-in ready 3-bedroom townhome on **Loretta Lane** sold for its asking price of **$659,900**. Newhall offers a unique and appealing blend of historic character and contemporary convenience, making it an attractive choice for a diverse range of homebuyers.

For those seeking an active adult lifestyle, the Friendly Valley 55+ community is a premier destination. This guard-gated community is rich with amenities, including a golf course, swimming pool, and clubhouse, making it a top choice for those 55 and over. In stark contrast, a one-of-a-kind single-story home on **Heritage Lane** in the highly coveted Happy Valley neighborhood sold for **$1,400,000**, surpassing its asking price of $1,395,000. This expansive property, situated on a nearly half-acre lot, includes a detached Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) with a full kitchen, bedroom, and bath.
