  ## 2&nbsp;&amp;dash;&nbsp;Why 714 Active Listings Don‚Äôt Mean Buyers Will Overpay

  ### 2.1 Sources of the Inventory Surge

  The jump from sub-600 listings in March to 714 today stems from three independent but overlapping realities:

  The ‚Äúbuyers are not stupid‚Äù mantra I repeated in today‚Äôs YouTube short rings truer than ever. Consumers run a six-tab workflow: Zillow + Redfin + Realtor.com + ChatGPT + YouTube walkthrough + hyper-local Facebook group. That workflow exposes:
