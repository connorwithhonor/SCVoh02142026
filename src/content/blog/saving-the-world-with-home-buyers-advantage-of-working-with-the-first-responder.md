We focused on the sold properties that closely matched the subject property. This included homes that were similar in size and features. It‚Äôs vital to use recent sales as the primary basis for any offer, as these properties have undergone the appraisal process and are considered validated sales.

In our scenario, there were 15 closings involving various loan types, including FHA, VA, and conventional loans. Understanding these dynamics helped us establish a stronger position.
