**Deeper Dive: Notable Market Observations**

	- 
	**Price Reductions vs. Market Health:**&nbsp;The significant number of price reductions across active listings isn't necessarily a sign of a collapsing market, but rather a market finding its footing. Sellers who priced aggressively based on past peak conditions are now adjusting to meet current buyer expectations and affordability constraints, influenced partly by interest rate sensitivity. This creates opportunities for savvy buyers.

	

	- 
	**DOM Spectrum:**&nbsp;The wide range in Days on Market highlights the importance of pricing and condition. Homes priced correctly based on&nbsp;current&nbsp;comparable sales and presented well are selling rapidly (often under 30 days). Properties lingering for months often require price adjustments or improvements, or they might be unique properties awaiting the right niche buyer.

	

	- 
	**Luxury Segment (&gt;$1.5M):**&nbsp;This segment shows activity, particularly in areas like Valencia, Stevenson Ranch, Agua Dulce, and Acton. However, DOM can be longer, and pricing needs to be well-supported by unique features, views, lot size, or exceptional condition.

	

	- 
	**Affordable Housing (Condos/Townhomes &lt;$600K):**&nbsp;This segment remains highly competitive across the SCV (especially in Canyon Country, Saugus, Newhall, and parts of Valencia). First-time buyers and investors are active here, often leading to quick sales when properties are listed competitively.
