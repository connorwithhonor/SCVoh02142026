## **What is Full Loan Approval, and How is It Different from Pre-Approval?**

There‚Äôs a major difference between **pre-qualification, pre-approval, and full loan approval**.

	- **Pre-Qualification:** A basic review of a buyer‚Äôs income and debt, often based on self-reported information. This is **not enough** to make a serious offer.

	- **Pre-Approval:** A lender reviews your financial documents (W-2s, bank statements, credit score) and issues a letter stating you qualify for a certain loan amount. However, this is **still conditional** and doesn‚Äôt guarantee final approval.

	- **Full Underwriting Approval:** The lender **verifies and approves everything**‚Äîincome, assets, employment, credit‚Äîbefore the buyer even finds a home. This makes the **loan as close to a cash offer as possible** because the only step left is the property appraisal.
