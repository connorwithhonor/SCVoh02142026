### 2. FHA Loan with 3.5% Down

	- 
	**Purchase Price:** $900,000

	

	- 
	**Down Payment (3.5%):** $31,500

	

	- 
	**Loan Amount:** $868,500

	

	- 
	**Interest Rate (Approx.):** 6.75% (varies daily)

	

	-

	- 
	**Monthly Principal &amp; Interest:** Approximately $5,633

FHA loans are popular because of their lenient credit requirements, but they do come with mortgage insurance premiums both upfront and monthly. A typical monthly MIP might add an extra few hundred dollars. The exact amount depends on your loan specifics (including loan amount and duration). As with the conventional loan scenario, property tax and insurance are on top of that, plus the HOA.

### 3. VA Loan with 0% Down

	- 
	**Purchase Price:** $900,000

	

	- 
	**Down Payment:** $0

	

	- 
	**Loan Amount:** $900,000

	

	- 
	**Interest Rate (Approx.):** 6.75% (varies)

	

	- 
	**Term:** 30 years

	

	- 
	**Monthly Principal &amp; Interest:** Approximately $5,837

	

	-

	-

VA loans typically do not require mortgage insurance, but most borrowers pay a VA funding fee (unless exempt). This fee can be financed into the loan, increasing the total principal owed, or paid in cash at closing. In turn, property taxes, insurance, and any HOA dues add on top of these estimates.

## Important Disclaimers

	- 
	**Interest Rate Variability**
	Rates can change daily. A small shift from 6.75% to 7% or down to 6.5% can significantly alter your monthly payment. Always consult your lender for a current interest rate quote.

	

	- 
	**Loan Qualification**
	Your credit history, debt-to-income ratio, employment stability, and down payment amount all figure into your eligibility for any of these programs. ‚ÄúAdvertised‚Äù rates often assume excellent credit (e.g., 740+ FICO) and a favorable debt-to-income ratio.

	

	-

	If you‚Äôre putting less than 20% down, expect either private mortgage insurance (PMI) on a conventional loan or mortgage insurance premium (MIP) on an FHA loan.
