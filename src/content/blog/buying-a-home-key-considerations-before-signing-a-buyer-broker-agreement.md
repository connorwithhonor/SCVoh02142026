### **Understanding the Buyer Broker Agreement**

**What is a Buyer Broker Agreement?**

A Buyer Broker Agreement is a contract between a buyer and a real estate broker that outlines the broker's duties, obligations, and compensation. This agreement ensures that the broker represents the buyer's interests in real estate transactions.
