Here are some interesting facts and statistics from the active real estate inventory in Santa Clarita Valley that potential buyers should know:

### 1. **Price Ranges**

	- Homes in the Santa Clarita Valley range widely in price, offering options for various budgets. For example:
	
		**Condos**: Prices range from around $400,000 for a 2-bedroom condo to over $1 million for larger townhomes or luxury condos.

		- **Single-family homes (SFR)**: The lower end starts around $500,000, with many homes in popular neighborhoods like Valencia and Canyon Country ranging from $700,000 to $900,000. Luxury homes can exceed $1.5 million, especially in newer developments or homes with large lots.

	

	

### 2. **Price Per Square Foot**

	- The price per square foot varies significantly depending on the neighborhood and property type:
	
		In **Valencia**, homes typically range from **$400 to $600 per square foot**, with some newer developments pushing closer to $700.

		- In **Canyon Country**, the average price per square foot is around **$400-$500**, depending on the size and features of the property.

		- **Luxury homes** or those with large lots in areas like **Stevenson Ranch** or **Newhall** can command over **$500-$700 per square foot**.

	

	

### 3. **Bedroom and Bathroom Counts**

	- The most common configurations in the Santa Clarita Valley are **3- to 4-bedroom** homes with **2 to 3 bathrooms**.

	
		**Condos and Townhomes**: Typically 2 to 3 bedrooms, and 2 bathrooms. For example, properties on **Flynn Drive** offer 2-bedroom, 2-bathroom configurations around 1,000 square feet.

		- **Single-family homes**: Most 3- to 4-bedroom homes come with 2 to 3 bathrooms, ranging from about 1,500 to 2,500 square feet. For example, homes on **Marisa Drive** in Canyon Country or **Windham Way** in Saugus fall into this category.

		- **Luxury homes** in areas like **Copper Hill or Hasley Canyon** tend to offer 5 or more bedrooms and 4+ bathrooms, often exceeding 3,000 square feet.

	

	

### 4. **Neighborhood Highlights**

	- 
	**Valencia**: Known for its master-planned communities, Valencia offers a balance of affordability and luxury. 	- 
	**Canyon Country**: A more affordable option with diverse housing types, Canyon Country offers homes like those on **Kimbrough Street** or **Camp Plenty Road**, which tend to be older but well-maintained, with larger lots.

	

	- 
	**Saugus**: A rapidly growing area, Saugus offers suburban living with many newer homes. Properties like those on **Windham Way** or **Alaminos Drive** feature 4-bedroom homes that cater to growing families.

	

	- 
	**Stevenson Ranch** and **Newhall**: These upscale neighborhoods feature luxury homes with spacious lots. Homes on **Wildwood Canyon Road** or **Vista Delgado Drive** offer some of the largest lots and most expansive homes in the area.

	

### 5. **Days on Market (DOM)**

	- Homes in high-demand areas like **Valencia** and **Saugus** tend to sell faster, with **average days on market ranging from 20 to 40 days**.

	
		For example, homes on **Iron Village Drive** in Valencia have been on the market for just a few weeks, signaling strong buyer interest.

	

	
	- In contrast, some properties in Canyon Country or **Hasley Canyon** may stay on the market longer, particularly if they are higher-priced or have unique features like large acreage. Properties on streets like **Sunny Ridge Terrace** or **Chestnut Lane** have DOM figures upwards of 50-60 days.

### 6. **Popular Features**

	- 
	**Pool homes** and **large backyards** are highly sought after, particularly in neighborhoods like **Plum Canyon** or **Stevenson Ranch**. These features often result in higher listing prices and quicker sales.

	

	- 
	**New construction**: Areas like **Five Knolls** or **Fair Oaks Ranch** are known for newer construction homes that offer modern amenities such as open floor plans, energy-efficient features, and smart home technology.
