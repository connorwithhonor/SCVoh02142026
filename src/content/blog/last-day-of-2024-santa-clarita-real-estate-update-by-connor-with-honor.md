## Briefing Doc: Santa Clarita Real Estate Market Analysis (12/13/2023 - 12/24/2024)

This briefing document reviews key themes and insights from real estate market data for Santa Clarita, CA, covering the period of 12/13/2023 to 12/24/2024. The analysis leverages data from multiple sources, including statistical sheets, market reports, and specific property listings.

**Overall Market Trends:**

	- **Active Market:** The Santa Clarita real estate market is currently active, with 427 active listings, indicating a robust market with potential opportunities for buyers and sellers.

	- **Median List Price:** The median list price across all property types is $825,000, reflecting a relatively high-value market.

	- **High Close Price Percentage:** A significant number of properties are closing at or near their list price. The overall close price percentage is 91.7%, showing strong seller leverage in the market.

	- **Varied Performance by Area:** Different areas within Santa Clarita exhibit varying market conditions, with some areas showing greater activity and price appreciation than others.

	- Data reveals a high percentage of properties closing at or above asking price, with a median close price percentage of 98.5% for single-family residences.

### Glossary of Key Terms

	- **List Price:** The initial asking price set by the seller for a property.

	- **Close Price:** The final price agreed upon by the buyer and seller for a property.

	- **Days on Market (DOM):** The number of days a property is actively listed for sale on the market.

	- **SP/LP:** The ratio of the selling price to the listing price, expressed as a percentage.

	- **BR:** The number of bedrooms in a property.

	- **BA:** The number of bathrooms in a property.

	- **SqFt:** The total square footage of the living space in a property.

	- **$/SqFt:** The price per square foot of a property, calculated by dividing the list price by the square footage.

	- **Carport Spaces:** The number of covered parking spaces available for a property.

	- **Year Built:** The year a property was constructed.

	- **Standard Status:** A classification indicating the current listing status of a property (e.g., "Coming Soon," "Active," "Pending," "Closed," "Hold," "Withdrawn").

	- **Property Type:** A categorization of properties based on their structural characteristics (e.g., single-family residence, townhouse, condominium).

	- **Market Area:** A geographical region used to segment the real estate market.

	- **Median:** The middle value in a dataset when arranged in numerical order.

	- **Average:** The sum of all values in a dataset divided by the number of values.
