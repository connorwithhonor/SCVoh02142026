### Chapter 1: The Transformation ‚Äì More Than Just Weight Loss

My journey to health was not merely about numbers on a scale; it was about reclaiming vitality, building resilience, and understanding the profound impact of discipline. This metamorphosis provided invaluable lessons that resonate deeply in my role as a Realtor.

#### The Genesis of Change: From LAPD to Leaner Living

For 17 years as an LAPD officer and another five as a reserve, my focus was on serving and protecting the community. The demanding nature of the job often meant irregular hours and stress, which can take a toll on personal health. Recognizing the need for a significant change, I embarked on a rigorous regimen of longer-term and intermittent fasting. This was not a quick fix, but a sustained commitment to a new lifestyle.

	- **Intermittent and Longer-Term Fasting:** This became the cornerstone of my weight loss. It taught me immense self-control, patience, and the body's remarkable ability to adapt and heal. The principles are simple: disciplined periods of eating followed by periods of fasting, allowing the body to tap into fat reserves. You can explore [my insights into what if fasting was the way we all torched off body fat and kept healthy](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.santaclaritaopenhouses.com/what-if-fasting-was-the-way-we-all-torched-off-body-fat-and-kept-healthy).

	- **Testosterone Therapy and Muscle Preservation:** As part of this transformation, I was able to start testosterone therapy, which was instrumental in helping me get into great shape and preserving muscle mass while shedding fat. This highlighted the importance of a holistic approach to health. It's crucial to understand that [if weight loss is your plan, make sure that weight is fat and not muscle](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.santaclaritaopenhouses.com/if-weight-loss-is-your-plan-make-sure-that-weight-is-fat-and-not-muscle).

	- **The Mindset Shift:** Beyond the physical aspects, this journey cultivated a profound mental fortitude. I learned that [fat torching on purpose is hard, but so is being fat](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.santaclaritaopenhouses.com/fat-torching-on-purpose-is-hard-but-so-is-being-fat). It instilled a deep belief that seemingly insurmountable goals are achievable with a clear plan, unwavering dedication, and the courage to start even if you are scared of the unknown. [Maybe you need to start your journey even if you are scared of the unknown](https://www.google.com/search?q=https://www.santaclaritaopenhouses.com/maybe-you-need-to-start-your-journey-even-if-you-are-scared-of-the-unknown).

#### Lessons Learned: Discipline, Persistence, and Strategic Planning

The process of losing 135 pounds was a masterclass in several key areas that are directly transferable to real estate:

	- **Discipline:** Sticking to a fasting regimen and fitness routine requires immense self-discipline. This is the same discipline I apply to every detail of your real estate transaction, ensuring no step is missed and every deadline is met.

	- **Persistence:** There were plateaus and challenges. The ability to push through, adjust strategies, and remain persistent is vital. In real estate, this means relentlessly pursuing your goals, even when faced with market fluctuations or negotiation hurdles.

	- **Strategic Planning:** Achieving significant weight loss requires careful planning ‚Äì meal timing, exercise routines, and understanding the body's metabolic responses. Similarly, a successful real estate transaction demands a meticulous plan, from pricing strategies to marketing execution.
