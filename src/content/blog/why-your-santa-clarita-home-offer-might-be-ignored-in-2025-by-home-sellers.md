### 3. Seller Responses: The Good, the Bad, and the Silent

Once your offer lands, sellers have four options:

	- **Accept**: They love your terms‚Äîprice, contingencies, timeline‚Äîand sign off. Rare in a hot market unless you‚Äôre offering cash at full price.

	- **Counter**: They propose new terms, like a higher price, shorter escrow, or fewer contingencies. You can accept, counter back, or walk away.

	- **Ignore**: No response at all. This happens when your offer isn‚Äôt competitive or they‚Äôre swamped with better ones.
