# **Boys to Men: How to Eat Like a Warrior and Build a Stronger Body ‚Äì Part 3**

You‚Äôve started moving. You‚Äôve begun to build discipline. You‚Äôre waking up earlier, lifting, walking, and embracing discomfort.

It‚Äôs time to go back to basics and eat **like a man was meant to eat.**

## **The One-Ingredient Rule**

If you want to build a strong, lean, and capable body, **follow this rule: Only eat foods with one ingredient.** That means:

	- **Meat** (Beef, Chicken, Pork, Lamb, Game Meat)

	- **Fish &amp; Seafood** (Salmon, Cod, Sardines, Shrimp, Oysters)

	- **Eggs** (Nature‚Äôs multivitamin)

	- **Vegetables** (As close to how they grow in nature)

	- **Fruits** (Rarely, and don‚Äôt peel them or juice them)

That‚Äôs it. No packaged junk. No "healthy" bars or protein shakes with a laundry list of chemicals. **Just real food.**

If it has a label with more than **one** ingredient? **It‚Äôs not food. It‚Äôs a science experiment.**

## **Meat: The Foundation of Strength**

For thousands of years, men built their bodies and survived on **animal foods.** Meat isn‚Äôt just protein‚Äîit‚Äôs packed with **vitamins, minerals, and essential fats** that make your brain and body function at their peak.

If you‚Äôre serious about getting stronger, leaner, and more focused, **meat should be your foundation.**

### **Best Choices:**

	- **Grass-fed Beef** (Steak, Ground Beef, Liver)

	- **Wild-Caught Fish** (Salmon, Sardines, Mackerel)

	- **Eggs** (Eat the yolks‚Äîthat‚Äôs where the nutrition is)

	- **Pastured Chicken &amp; Pork**

Most food in stores isn‚Äôt **food**‚Äîit‚Äôs a chemical formula designed to make you addicted and keep you coming back for more.

	- **Seed oils** (Canola, Soybean, Corn Oil) cause inflammation and wreck hormones.

	- **Sugar** (Hidden in almost everything) spikes insulin and makes you fat.

	- **Artificial ingredients** (Flavors, colors, preservatives) hijack your brain‚Äôs reward system.

If you‚Äôve been eating junk your whole life, quitting won‚Äôt be easy. But after **two weeks of eating only real food,** you‚Äôll feel a massive difference in your energy, mood, and strength.

Vegetables are great‚Äîbut not all are created equal. Some are packed with **anti-nutrients** that can cause gut issues if eaten in excess. Stick to **low-toxin** vegetables and **fermented** options for gut health.

### **Best Vegetables:**

	- **Leafy Greens** (Spinach, Kale, Romaine)

	- **Cruciferous Veggies** (Broccoli, Cauliflower, Brussels Sprouts)

	- **Fermented Vegetables** (Sauerkraut, Kimchi)

Fruit isn‚Äôt bad‚Äîit‚Äôs just not meant to be eaten in **massive** quantities like today‚Äôs world promotes. Keep it **whole** (never juice it) and eat the **skin**‚Äîthat‚Äôs where the fiber is.

### **Best Fruits:**

	- **Berries** (Blueberries, Blackberries, Raspberries)

	- **Apples (with the skin on)**

	- **Oranges (Eat them, don‚Äôt drink them)**

	- **Avocados** (Yes, they‚Äôre a fruit)

	- **Your hunger disappears.** Processed foods make you **crave more**. Real food keeps you **satisfied.**

	- **Your energy skyrockets.** No more crashes, brain fog, or bloating.
