#### Current Market Overview

As of today, we have 464 active listings on the market in all of Santa Clarita Valley. Our inventory has been slowly increasing over the past few weeks, which is noteworthy considering the decline we experienced for quite some time. For instance, in June 2021, we saw similarly high inventory levels, indicating a potential shift in the market dynamics.

In the past four weeks, we were at around 420-430 units for sale. This gradual increase in inventory might suggest a market leveling out if the buyer demand does not increase correspondingly. We could potentially see a transition from a seller's market to a more balanced market, and possibly into a buyer's market if the inventory continues to rise without a corresponding increase in buyer activity.

	- **Hold**: 10 listings are on hold, meaning they are temporarily off the market but might return soon.

	- **Withdrawn**: 4 listings were withdrawn, similar to canceled but often for longer-term reasons.

#### Understanding Market Trends and Their Impact

The current increase in inventory and price adjustments suggest a shift in the market dynamics. As more properties become available and prices are adjusted, buyers have more options and possibly more negotiating power. This trend is crucial for both buyers and sellers to understand:

	- **For Sellers**: Pricing your home competitively is more important than ever. As inventory rises, buyers have more choices, and overpriced homes may linger on the market longer. Consider professional appraisals and comparative market analyses to set a realistic and attractive price.

	- **For Buyers**: With increasing inventory, now might be a great time to find a home that meets your needs. Be sure to get pre-approved for a mortgage to strengthen your position in negotiations.
