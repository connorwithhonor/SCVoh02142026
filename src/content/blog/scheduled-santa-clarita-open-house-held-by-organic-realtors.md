The real estate industry has long been influenced by large platforms that offer paid placements and lead generation services. While these platforms provide valuable exposure, they come with several drawbacks:

Paid placements can be expensive, especially for independent agents. High costs for advertising on platforms like Zillow or Google Ads can eat into an agent‚Äôs profits, making it challenging to offer competitive rates or invest in other aspects of their business.

### 4. **Dependency on Platforms**
