The market's upward trend in listings could mean a potential shift in market dynamics. Interest rates might see a change in September, which could further impact the buying cycle. Lower rates could lead to a massive buying cycle, moving through the current inventory quickly and potentially returning to a highly competitive market with multiple offers on properties.
