#### Additional Insights

The market's upward trend in listings could mean a potential shift in market dynamics. Interest rates might see a change in September, which could further impact the buying cycle. If rates drop significantly, it could create a surge in buying activity, reminiscent of previous market conditions with multiple offers on properties.

If you are considering purchasing real estate and you're able to qualify, it might be wise to act before any significant interest rate drops. Lower rates could lead to a massive buying cycle, moving through the current inventory quickly and potentially returning to a highly competitive market with multiple offers on properties.
