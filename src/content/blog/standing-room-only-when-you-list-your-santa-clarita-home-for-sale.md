### **Real-Life Example: Roof Repairs During a Connor with Honor Experience**

	- Recently, I conducted a listing presentation for sellers in Northpark. While they decided not to sell until 2025, this experience is worth mentioning:
	
		The sellers were considering a sale, either now or later. I coordinated with my team and arranged an appointment with the homeowners.

		- The home was a 3,300-square-foot, two-story property with RV parking and a large, terraced backyard with stunning views. My team immediately got to work, assessing the property and preparing recommendations.

		- One recommendation came from Steve Belgau, a respected roofer in Santa Clarita, CA. He discovered two tears in the roofing paper, which could cause leaks if it rained. The sellers decided to delay listing until 2025, but they got the roof repaired. A week later, it rained heavily, confirming the wisdom of their decision.

		- **Was it worth it? Absolutely.** Even if you choose not to sell after my presentation, you may learn something valuable that saves you stress and money in the future.
