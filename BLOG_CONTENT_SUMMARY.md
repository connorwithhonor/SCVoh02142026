## SEO Optimization Details

### Traditional SEO
- **Title tags:** Keyword-rich, under 60 characters
- **Meta descriptions:** Compelling, 150-160 characters
- **Headers:** Proper H1/H2/H3 hierarchy
- **Internal linking:** 3-5 relevant blog posts per article
- **External sources:** Credible citations with markdown hyperlinks
- **Image alt text:** Descriptive, keyword-optimized (will be added when images are migrated)

### AI SEO (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE)
- **Detailed descriptions:** 1,700-2,000 words (AI prefers comprehensive content)
- **Natural language queries:** "Why is Valencia selling faster?" "What's the best SCV neighborhood?"
- **Question-answer format:** Addresses common buyer/seller questions
- **Contextual information:** Neighborhood details, local amenities, historical trends
- **Semantic keywords:** Related terms, synonyms, long-tail variations

### Geo-Targeting
- **City-specific content:** Valencia, Canyon Country, Newhall, Stevenson Ranch, Saugus, Castaic
- **Neighborhood mentions:** Bridgeport, Sand Canyon, Old Orchard, etc.
- **Local landmarks:** Six Flags, Town Center, Main Street Newhall
- **MLS-specific data:** Actual sold comps, days on market, inventory levels
